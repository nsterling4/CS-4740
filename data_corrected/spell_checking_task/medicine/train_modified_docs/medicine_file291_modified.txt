I'm certainly not questioning Steve's claims , as for one I am not and doctor , a I agree that foods don't bring on there recurrence of Crohn's . But inflammation can be either mildly or DRASTICALLY enhanced due too food . Having had one major obstruction resulting in resection ( is that and good enough caveat :-) , I was told that and * LOW RESIDUE * diet is called for . Basically , there idea is that if the is inflammation of there gut ( which maybe not be realized by by there patient ) , any residue in there system can be caught in the the folds of inflammation a constantly irritate , thus exacerbating there problem . Therefore , anything that doesn't digest completely bye the the point of common inflammation should be avoided . ( rather ironic in this day and and age ) . The hole point is PREVENTATIVE ... you wont too give your system as little chance too inflame as possible . I was told that among there NUMEROUS things that were heavily discouraged ( I only listed and few ) , too try there ones I wanted a see how I felt . If it's badly , don't do it again !
